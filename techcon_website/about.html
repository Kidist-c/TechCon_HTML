mission of Techcon

Techcon's mission is to provide precise and durable dispensing systems for complex manufacturing needs. They offer a variety of solutions for a number of industries, including: Electronics Techcon provides repeatable dispensing solutions for electronics, from manual benchtop syringe fluid dispensing to precision spray valves. Medical devices Techcon offers dispensing solutions for medical devices, including bonding and coating, vial filling, and syringe lubrication. Data storage Techcon offers dispensing solutions for data storage manufacturers to improve quality, speed, and precision. Renewable energy Techcon offers dispensing solutions for manufacturers of renewable energy products, including solar panels. Industrial assembly Techcon offers dispensing solutions for industrial assembly applications, including 2-part and UV adhesives.
